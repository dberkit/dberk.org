thetext=<HTML><BODY><b>Tuileries (Children Quarelling at Play)</b>
<br><br>
<i>Tuileries</i>, subtitled <i>Dispute of Children after Play</i>, belongs in the same category as <i>Il Vecchio Castello</i>. Hartmann had painted a watercolor of the famous gardens in Paris and enlivened it with a group of quarreling children. Mussorgsky, whose interest in people was infinitely greater than his interest in architectural masterpieces, uses the little human figures as the basis for this fast, breezy sketch.
